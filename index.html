How the peg board works

The board is laid out as a triangle of staggered pegs. Each row is wider than the one above it, so the ball has a fresh left-or-right choice at every step. That creates a natural spread of outcomes, with the centre hit far more often than the edges.

In simple terms, the board acts like a Galton board. More paths lead toward the middle, while the outer pockets are harder to reach. That is why casino betticasinos games tend to keep small central values and place larger multipliers at the sides.
